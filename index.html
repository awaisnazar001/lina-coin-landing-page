How to Buy?


Quick Start Guide


1

Create Metamask Wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive LINA.


2

Send BNB to your wallet

You can buy (BNB) directly on MetaMask or transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, etc. Make sure to use the BEP-20 network when transfering BNB.


3

Connect your wallet to linacoin.eu

Access your wallet to linacoin.eu by clicking ‘Connect’ on the Pre-sale page and select MetaMask from there.


4

Participate in Pre-sale & Swap your BNBs for LINA

You can participate in the Pre-sale as soon as you have BNB available! Press ‘Participate now’ and enter the amount in BNB of how much you want to buy LINA and you're done.
